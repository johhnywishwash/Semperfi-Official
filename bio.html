  • OUR STORY SO FAR ...

  • Formed in late 2008 in the city of Aberdeen (Scotland), Semperfi have effectively created the foundations of a strong, solid Metal band. Since their early beginnings, Semperfi have fashioned their own unique Metal sound by blending many musical influences - and not just from the Metal genre. Each member's musical tastes are as wide and varied as the next, which adds depth, diversity and a wide spectrum of colours to Semperfi's song writing and creative flair.

    Only being 3 years old, Semperfi have attained many noteworthy achievements. In 2009 they recorded and released the 3-track EP 'The Abyss' which gained them widespread recognition and a great amount of praise. In 2010 they gigged up and down Scotland relentlessly - including an appearance at Shetland's 'Vunk' festival - in order to build up a substantial fan base and to make a vast amount of music industry contacts. In 2011 Semperfi filmed and released their first music video for the single 'Sick of You', and subsequently supported bands such as Japan's 'Electric Eel Shock' and the Essex Metal band 'Forever Never'. They also began recording their debut album 'INSIDE' which is due to be released in early 2012. They embarked upon a 2-week UK tour in the Summer of 2011, which saw them play and receive a rapturous welcome from cities such as Manchester, Leeds, Newcastle, Edinburgh, Dundee and their home city of Aberdeen. To finish the tour they travelled to the island of Shetland to appear in 'Vunk' festival yet again - this time to headline the Friday night.

    Semperfi have many things planned for 2012 and beyond - the release of their debut album 'INSIDE', supporting the critically- acclaimed and world renowned act CHIMAIRA on the Aberdeen leg of their UK tour, and a UK + European tour of their own lined up for Summer - are just a few things on the horizon for Semperfi.
